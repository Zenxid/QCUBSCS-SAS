Hello, [Student Name]! You can now access your test permit.


To download your test permit, click "Download Admission Test Permit" button below.


Download and review your test permit. Please ensure that all the details are correct.


Kindly refer to the following instructions for all exam-related information.


A. Bring two (2) copies of originally-signed test permits with attached 2x2 ID picture.

B. Don't forget your test permit on your exam day. Examinees WITHOUT their exam permit will not be permitted to take the test.

C. Arrive at least 30 minutes before your exam schedule. Examinees should expect heavy traffic around the school premises.

D. Late examinees will NOT be allowed to take the test.

E. Bring your school ID or any government-issued with picture.

F. Strictly use No. 2 Mongol pencil. Usage of any other type of pencil will be prohibited.

G. Be in proper attire. Wear t-shirt, polo or blouse, long pants, or below the knee skirt.

H. If you have any medical condition that requires special attention, please inform the Admission Office ahead of the time to ensure your safety during the exam. You may reach us out at admission.fu.edu.ph.


Good luck, future Falcons!
